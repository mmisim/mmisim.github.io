Standard Cake Pricing

  • 8-inch, 3-layer cakes: starting at $75 (serves 10-16 people)
  • 10-inch, 3-layer cakes: starting at $95 (serves 20-30 people)
  • 12-inch, 3-layer cakes: starting at $145 (serves 25-35 people)
  • 1/4-sheet, 3-layer cakes: starting at $125 (serves 30-35 people)

Prices do not include tax or delivery. Some customizations, such as fondant, may incur additional charges. Pleasecontact Samantha for a personalized quote.


Wedding Cake Pricing

Wedding cakes start at $6 per serving, plus tax and delivery, and some minimum orders apply during peak months and for delivery outside of New York City. Pricing details and options will be available during your tasting. There is also a $75 tasting fee for weddings, which is waived with your order of $600 or more.

Please see Weddings for more information on the wedding cake process, or contact Samantha for a personalized quote.
